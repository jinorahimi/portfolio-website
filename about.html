About

My Story

I’m a UX designer with a background in arts administration, research, and education and experience in a variety of nonprofit and commercial organizations. I have over 6 years of experience working directly with artists and audiences from diverse backgrounds in the Middle East, Europe, and North America. In my previous roles, I supported art institutions in developing exhibitions, public programs, publications, international collaborations, and community engagement strategies that forge productive relationships between artists and their publics.

My experience working with diverse audiences inspired me to pursue a career in UX Design and focus on designing solutions that respond to user’s needs. The work that I do as an arts administrator, researcher, and UX designer are inextricably intertwined and inform each other. My background as a curator and educator strengthens my critical thinking, arms me with empathic listening skills, and prepares me to develop products and services that are engaging and responsive.

I hope we can connect soon!
